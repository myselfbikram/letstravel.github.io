5 Must Visit Places in India



India is a vast country. Rich in culture and tradition, India is truly a place to explore before you die. To truly experience the culture and history of India, Travelling is neccessary. Here we will talk about 5 most beautiful places in India that you must visit.


5. AMRITSAR

Amritsar is located in the Northern province of Punjab. Mostly known for it's Golden Temple, which is also known as Harmandir Sahib. Amritsar is one of the holy city in India and a mecca for the Sikh Religion in the country. Golden Temple in Amritsar was built 400 years back and truly one of the place that you must visit before you die.


4. GOA

Goa is located on the Western Coast in India. It's a former Portugese Colony and that is why it still has the colonial influences all over. It is one of the most tourist attract places in India. Goa beaches is well known even among the foreign people who visits in large number every year. Candolim Beach is one of the most popular beach in Goa where large number tourist flock to soak up the Sun. Palolem and Anjuna beach are also among the beautiful beaches in Goa but little less crowded. Chapora Fort is also a place to walk around in Goa.


3.LADAKH

The mountainous destination of Ladakh is located in the Northernmost India and is considered no less than heaven. People from around the globe admires this beautiful gift to Earth. Ladakh is one such place to visit in Kashmir which would give you a lifetime experience with it's breathtaking and pristine scenery. Travellers all around the globe loves to visit the Buddhist Monasteries in this old town.


02.NEW DELHI

They say, Delhi is one such place in India where you should visit taking time in your hand. Starting from Red Fort to Qutub Minar, Delhi is a place that would truly blow your mind. New Delhi is one of the busiest place in India and also the Capital of India. Moreover In Delhi, One must make time for many museums and religious structures that make up the city.


01.MYSORE

Mysore is located in the southernmost tip of India and is one of the most well known place among the travellers all around because of it's magnificent Mysore Palace. This palace is a epitome of opulence, and the structure reveals upscale touches like carved rosewood doors, ceilings decorated in ivory and countless paintings on the walls. Devaraja Market is also one such place in Mysore which you won't prefer to miss.

Top 5 Foreign Destinations



The bonding of the Earth and the mankind have co-created some of the most spectacular places on this Blue Planet. From cities to Beaches, From Mountains to spectacular national parks, it is hard to keep your bucket list stagnant. Keeping in mind the iconic tourist spots, culture, food and ease of travel, we have put this top 5 foreign destinations that you must visit!


5. MAURITIUS

One of the basic reason why you must be planning your next trip to Mauritius is that you will never get bored when visiting Mauritius. Being termed as the pearl of Indian Ocean, Mauritius will blow up your mind with it's heavenly beaches and the palm trees surrounding it. There are over a hundred beaches along the coastlines of this island. People who likes sea diving, would surely experience how much the island is blessed with natural assets. Tropical climate, wild waterfalls, lush forestation, mountain ranges, water sports, the island is a holiday destination with endless possibilities.


4. CAPE TOWN

Cape Town is located on the southwestern tip of the African Continent. Starting from the vibrant city centre to urban mountains, from pristine beaches to wine farms, Cape Town has everything to make your trip one that you remember for your lifetime. Cape Town’s mediterranean climate is sublime mostly in the summer months. Table Mountain which acts as the ultimate backdrop to the city, is a beautiful, large, flat-topped mountain. Cape Town is a ultimate choice of a place if you are an adventurous soul and you love mountain hiking.


3.MALDIVES

Making it to Maldives is no longer hard and unachievable these days. Maldives is one of the most picture-perfect countries in the world. With a panoramic view of the turquoise blue water all around and the blissful twilight hours with thousand hues of red, orange and purple, Maldives is truly magical. Moreover, the sight of Bodu Beru, the popular Maldivian folk dance which is often held during evening on the beach is equally charming and a chance for you to know the locals.


02.NEW YORK

Selecting a travel destination in the United States can be challenging at times. This country has numerous tourist hotspots which can take weeks for you to decide what exactly you need to explore. However, one of the most quintessential cities you need to visit at least once in your lifetime is none other than the New York City. This place is filled up with incredible landmarks and attractions which are all accessible via cab, bus, or train. Nevertheless, there are a handful of foods that have become staples in a New Yorker’s diet. One must have heard New York is “The City That Never Sleeps", which is named after it's delightful night scenes.


01.SYDNEY

Among Australia's many other vacation spots, Sydney is the primary destination which deserves your time and presence. The big thing in Sydney is, without doubt, the Sydney Opera House. It is beautifully located on Sydney Harbour, and is one of Australia's World Heritage sites. Sydney Harbour Bridge is also one such iconic hotspot which makes Sydney as one of the ultimate travelling destination of all time.

Things to pack while travelling



Packing can be tricky, but we’ve made this a little less tiresome for you. We have devised a total packing list. So even if you have got a big trip coming up? Use this ultimate guide to remind you of useful essentials you need to have in your backpack starting from the shower gel, socks, clothes or sunnies. So Knowing how to pack a backpack for a long trip is no more a hard thing to do. Just simply treat this as a packing checklist and tick off each item as you go. Of course, it also depends on what kind of trip you’re about to embark on whether it’s trekking a mountain, or a holiday near the beach side, We’ve tried to include items that cater to every kind of trip!

1. PASSPORT

Passport is one of the first thing you should pack while you plan to travel or else you won't be getting any far without it. Alongside that, one must make sure the passport is valid too. It's always good to have at least six months on your passport before it expires so always check your expiry date so that you have a enough time to issue a new one before the trip comes.

2.MOBILE CHARGER

Forgetting Mobile Charger when you are in rush is a common thing these days. Buying one in the airport can be pretty expensive. So we make sure you never leave your charger back at your home and never miss a chance to charge your Smartphone.

3.EXTRA PAIR OF SHOE

Packing an extra pair of Shoe can always come handy when you're planning for a long trip. You never know when the unexpected happens and you face an ugly wet weather. If you'll be doing a lot of walking or hiking then having a good quality walking shoes make it easier. It’s usually best to wear your bulkiest shoes at the airport so that you can save some space in your luggage.

4.FIRST-AID STUFF

It's always good to have some medication on the road. When you're having a long hectic schedule of travelling and a tiring journey, Painkillers and allergy tablets will come in handy for headaches, muscle aches, period pains, stomach aches and so on. Also having few band-aid in the First-Aid kit is always a good move.

5.CAMERA

In modern day travelling, who doesn't love to show some photography skills? ALso to keep the memories updated and posting beautiful moments on one's social media account, Camera has become one of the most important thing to pack in.

Things not to miss on a trip



People in modern days have become more adventurous and excited towards travelling. They never miss a chance to explore new things when on a trip. Travelling is lot more than just a sightseeing these days. Here are the few exciting stuffs that you can do while you are out for a trip.

1.MOUNTAIN HIKING

Mountain Hiking has become one of the most common thing to do on a trip these days. The young and sporty souls these days are more fond of a hiking as it helps to live the moment and also reduce stress. Moreover, Hiking with friends and sharing the experience can bring you closer together to form stronger bonds and create lasting memories.

2.AIR-BALLOON RIDE

Hot air balloon rides are truly a special experience. Everyone always talks about what it’s like once you’re up in the air, an air-balloon ride indeed helps you to feel that moment and emotion. Can you imagine how unbelievable it is from the sky to look everything down the ground? Such special event is ideal for a birthday gift, romantic date, wedding event and so on.

3.SCUBA DIVING

One should never miss a chance to go for a scuba diving and explore the beautiful underwater world. During scuba diving you learn about underwater life. You also explore areas, which are difficult to visit and relatively few people come.

Food to have on a trip



Planning a fun trip is always exciting but what one must make sure is to pack some good healthy and nutritious food which is neccessary to keep you active and fun-filled throughout your journey. Let's see some of the food that you can store on your backpack while travelling.

1.VEGGIES AND FRUITS

Packing some veggies like Cauliflower florets, Broccoli florets, Baby carrots, Snap peas and Radishes is always a good option to keep you healthy. Fruits like Apple, Cherries and Clementines which are not messy and stays fresh for a long is also a good idea to pack on.

2.DRY FRUITS

Dry-Fruits are easier stuff to carry while travelling. Some of the dry-fruits that you can carry while you're on a trip are almonds, raisins, cashews, dates, figs, apricots, peaches and pears.

3.CHOCOLATES AND DRINKS

Who doesn't love to bite a chunk of chocolate on a long tiring ride? Dark Chocolate tends to keep you active, adding to it, Smoothie and Banana Shake helps you to keep hydrated with sufficient protein.
