Overseas advance payment can be made through Money order to our bank.

REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY:
In the event of cancellation of tour / travel services due to any avoidable / unavoidable reason/s we must be notified of the same in writing. Cancellation charges will be effective from the date we receive advice in writing, and cancellation charges would be as follows:

30 days prior to arrival: 5% of the service cost.
15 days prior to arrival: 15% of the service cost.
07 days prior to arrival: 50% of the service cost.
48 hours prior to arrival OR No Show: No Refund

Note:
Written cancellation will accept only on working days, except Saturday and Sunday, Any cancellation sent on Sunday's will be considered on next working day (Monday).

For the X-mas and new year period from 20 Dec to 05 Jan the payment is non-refundable.

In case you cancel the trip after commencement, refund would be restricted to a limited amount only which too would depend on the amount that we would be able to recover from the hoteliers/ contractors we patronize. For unused hotel accommodation, chartered transportation & missed meals etc. we do not bear any responsibility to refund.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS-

Travel agency is one of the most hired service in recent days. We often have queries in our mind before contacting travel agents. Here are few of our most commonly asked questions that we have put together to set your mind at ease.

HOW DO TRAVEL AGENTS GET PAID?

Travel Agents get paid by the resorts and the hotels, Travel Agents never charge any extra or hidden service fee.

WHAT ONE MUST DO BEFORE CONTACTING TRAVEL AGENTS?

Firstly, one should set a budget and share it with us, so that we can give you a best package as per as your budget that you are comfortable with.

DO I HAVE TO PAY THE PACKAGE AMOUNT UP FRONT?

Not at all! Money is not our main goal. Our goal is customer satisfaction. So we understand your concern and we have kept easy break ups through which you can pay us the package amount in 3 easy steps which will not pinch your pocket but help you to enjoy your trip.

CAN I TRAVEL FOREIGN COUNTRIES WITHOUT PASSPORT?

Technically No! We highly recommend you to carry a valid passport also a valid Id Card. In case if something went wrong, like a health issue, you have to be taken to a medical facility, you may encounter challenges during that period.
